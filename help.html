How To Play Minesweeper

Mines: 🐶
Flags: 🐾

Objective

Each square can either contain a mine or be empty. Avoid clicking on a square with a mine—this will end the game!

Starting the Game

The first click is always safe (no mines). Click on a square to reveal what’s beneath it.

Numbers on the Grid

If you click an empty square, you may see a number. This number indicates how many mines are in the squares surrounding it. Use these numbers to deduce where the mines are hidden!

Marking Mines

Right-click on a square to mark it with a flag if you think it contains a mine. This helps you keep track of suspected mine locations.

Clearing Nearby Squares

If a number is revealed and you’ve flagged the correct number of surrounding mines, you can clear the remaining squares around it by clicking the number again. Caution: If you’ve flagged incorrectly, you may trigger a mine!

Game End

You Win By:

- Revealing all non-mine squares
- Flagging all mines

You Lose By:

Uncovering a mine :(
